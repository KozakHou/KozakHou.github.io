Kozak Hou

Applied CSE, Imperial College London

I am a master’s student supervised by Professor Naoto Tsujii (University of Tokyo) and coadvised by Dr. Marijan Beg (Imperial College London) in the MSc Applied Computational Science and Engineering program at Imperial College London. My research interests are in applied science via high performance computing. Recently, I have been working on accelerating Two-Dimensional Lower Hybrid Wave code via Physics-Informed Neural Operator on TST-2 spherical tokamak located at University of Tokyo, Kashiwa Campus.

Prior to my master’s, I completed my bachelor’s degree in Space Science and Engineering with a credit program in Interdicipline Artificial Intilligence at National Central University in Taiwan (2020-2024). I was privileged to be advised by Chun-Yu Lin and Jih-Hong Shue for their guidance in high-performance scientific computing and numerical methods in space physics.
